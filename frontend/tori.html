Welcome

Hey guys! I’m Victoria Calles, the voice behind this fabulous little site. I’m a 23 year old hairdresser living in beautiful Chicago, Illinois. I absolutely love what I do and in January of 2016, I launched this website to help inspire other women in their beauty journey. My hope was to teach all about the science behind how certain things work the way they do (like why highlights pulls brassy easily), to answer your burning questions (like why your hair could be falling out) and to just inspire and motivate you to learn to love and embrace your hair and the beautiful woman that wears it!

In my free time (I know, right? What free time?), I love to read, drink wine, indulge in reality tv, travel to the beach, practice yoga, run, dance, sing, meditate and spend as much time with my amazing girlfriends as possible. I absolutely love the life I have because I created it that way and I believe in waking up with positive purpose everyday. During my days, there’s a lot of laughter and smiling and gratitude and I wouldn’t have it any other way.
